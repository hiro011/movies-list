About

Movie-List is a simple, local web application that lets you manage a personal library of your favorite movies. You can add, edit, delete, and rearrange movies with custom names, images, and links. No login or internet connection is needed after loading the site.

Storage

All your data is stored in your browser using Local Storage, meaning:

  • Your list is saved only on your device
  • The data stays even after closing the browser
  • Clearing browser storage will erase your movie list

Settings

Use the Settings page to customize your experience:

  • Change the navigation background image
  • Set a default image and default link for new movies
  • Choose from multiple color themes
